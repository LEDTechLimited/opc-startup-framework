The 7 Layers

A complete operating stack — from vision to retention.

Build inside-out. Each layer locks before the next opens. The discipline is what creates the moat.

L1

Brand Foundation

Lock vision, 5 strategic pillars, anchor decisions resolving open contradictions. The authoritative reference for everything that follows.

L2

Positioning & ICP

Tier-by-tier customer personas, hero product per tier, messaging matrix. First-principle drivers, not surface needs.

L3

Visual & Design System

Design principles, color, typography, components — serving Layer 2 positioning, not imposing aesthetic agenda.

L4

Website & Product UX

Information architecture, user journeys per tier, AI interactions. Idiot-proof hardware UX. Apple-style software simplicity.

L5

Content & SEO/GEO

Cold-keyword strategy. Content matrix. ChatGPT / Perplexity / AI Overview optimization. Cold keywords × N = niche demand.

L6

Channel & Campaign Mix

Practitioner-voice hooks. YouTube / LinkedIn / paid ads. Voice constraint: no marketing-speak, only specific.

L7

Sales Funnel & Retention

Consultative sales flow. Referral mechanism designed around loss-aversion. Retention quality is what builds the category-defining brand. The long game.
