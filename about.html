About

A founder operating system, distilled from the books that actually worked.

What this is

The OPC Startup Framework is a complete operating system for solo founders and small teams in the AI era. It addresses the gap between disconnected tactical advice ("hire a content marketer", "run Facebook ads", "post on LinkedIn") and abstract strategic theory.

You answer ~30 questions. You get a Master Blueprint. Your AI co-pilot loads it as primary context. Strategic coherence — locked.

Why "OPC"

OPC = One-Person Company. Even if you're not running a literal one-person operation today, the operating principles apply: build for independence, recurring revenue, transferable value. Your business should be an asset, not a job.

What it's distilled from

  • "一人AI公司" (One-Person AI Company) — Operating model, three growth paths, the Golden Triangle Loop, market antenna systems, exit-readiness principles
  • Hormozi's $100M Offers + $100M Money Models — Starving Crowd validation, Value Equation, four profit levers, Sales as Selection mindset
  • Power.ai 7-Prompt Founder Framework — Tactical prompt library mapped to specific framework layers
  • Real-world implementation experience — The framework was built and stress-tested on actual businesses before being abstracted into this generic version

What it's NOT

  • A SaaS-specific playbook (try Lenny's Newsletter)
  • A growth-hack list
  • A consumer app virality strategy
  • A startup accelerator curriculum
  • Magic — it's just a coherent stack that prevents the most common founder mistakes

Who it's for

You'll get the most out of this framework if you're:

  • A solo founder or 1-3 person team
  • Selling into a niche with real customers (B2B, premium B2C, professional services)
  • Using AI tools daily (Claude / ChatGPT / Gemini / Cursor)
  • Playing for a 3-10 year horizon
  • Tired of scattered tactical advice and want a coherent foundation

How to use it

  1. Read the Framework Reference (~30 min)
  2. Build your Master Blueprint (~60-90 min)
  3. Configure your AI co-pilot (Claude Project / Custom GPT / Gemini Gem)
  4. Execute Layer 2 → Layer 7 with strategic coherence
  5. Run the quarterly Golden Triangle Loop to keep the engine alive

Privacy

Your answers are stored locally in your browser only — never sent anywhere. The site has no backend, no analytics, no tracking. You can clear your browser data to reset.
